Service

Customer Engagement:

Greet and seat customers promptly with a friendly welcome.
Provide menus and offer suggestions or specials when appropriate.
Take orders accurately, pay attention to any dietary restrictions,
and answer questions about the menu.

Table Etiquette:

Train waitstaff in proper table service etiquette, including setting the table, serving food, and clearing dishes. Maintain a clean and organized dining area throughout the meal. Address any spillages or issues promptly and discreetly.

Timely Service:

Ensure efficient and timely delivery of food to the table.
Coordinate the timing of courses to avoid long waits and ensure a smooth dining experience.

Food Knowledge:

Staff should be knowledgeable about the menu, including ingredients, preparation methods, and potential allergens. Recommend suitable wine or beverage pairings with various dishes.

Customer Satisfaction:

Be attentive to customer feedback and concerns, and handle any complaints or issues with professionalism and empathy. Thank customers for their patronage and invite them to return.
